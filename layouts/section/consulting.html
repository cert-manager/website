Production Readiness Review

A Jetstack Production Readiness Review will validate existing work on Kubernetes, and identify any areas of your cluster that could be improved.

You will receive a report with improvements laid out in a suggested remediation plan with recommendations prioritised based on criticality. By the end of the review, you will have a list of concrete steps on getting to a best-practice cluster.

In the process of delivering Production Readiness reports for customers, Jetstack have uncovered a wide variety of issues that would otherwise have proved critical to the production usage of Kubernetes. We have also saved up to 45% of cloud bills by accurately sizing and architecting the cluster.

  • Day 1 On-site cluster review
  • Day 2 On-site cluster review
  • Within 15 days A full and comprehensive Production Readiness report, delivered on-site

Cloud Native Discovery Workshop

A Cloud Native Discovery Workshop is a comprehensive assessment of the overall status of your infrastructure and its application with a plan for moving to a Cloud Native architecture on Kubernetes.

You will receive a full migration plan with all steps laid out. This migration plan will help with the setup of a Kubernetes platform and migration of your application to it.

  • Day 1 Scoping & Best Practices
  • Day 2 Architecture & Planning
  • Within 15 days A full comprehensive migration plan with an architectural overview
