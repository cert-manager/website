Why join us?

We are a fast-growing Kubernetes professional services company that prides itself with helping startups and enterprises alike in their path to modern cloud-native infrastructure. Since early 2015 we have been partnering with companies to accelerate their cloud journey by building trusted, secure and scalable Kubernetes clusters that stand up in production.

We have a strong brand and well-regarded reputation in the community. We also have personal relationships with many of the major cloud vendors, and a growing customer base. We offer a culture of flexible working, and enable you to work on either solutions-focused consultancy helping companies solve complex technical issues, or product engineering - developing open source tools extending the Kubernetes platform and giving back to the community. You would join a world-class team, play a leading part in our customers’ success, and the adoption of our technology in the open source community.
